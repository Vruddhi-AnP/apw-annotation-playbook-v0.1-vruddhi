Edge Cases and Handling

Edge Case 1: Mixed sentiment
   Example:
      - Not bad, but not great

Handling:
   - Mark as Neutral because sentiment is mixed.

Edge Case 2: Mild or factual statements
    Example:
    - It works fine

Handling:
    - Mark as Neutral as there is no strong emotion.

Edge Case 3: Average experience
    Example:
     - Customer service was average

Handling:
    - Mark as Neutral unless strong positive or negative words are used.

General Rule:
    -If the sentiment is confusing or unclear, choose Neutral and move forward.
