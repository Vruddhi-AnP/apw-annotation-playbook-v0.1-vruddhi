Purpose:
    To explain how to label text data as Positive, Negative, or Neutral.

Labels and When to Use Them:

Positive
    -Use this label when the sentence shows happiness, satisfaction, or appreciation.

Examples:
- I love this product
- Absolutely fantastic experience

Negative
    -Use this label when the sentence shows complaint, anger, or dissatisfaction.

Examples:
- This is the worst service ever
- I will never use this again

Neutral
     -Use this label when the sentence is factual, mixed, or not clearly positive or negative.

Examples:
- The delivery was okay
- Customer service was average

- If confused, mark as Neutral and note it as an edge case
